You’re about to treat your palate with great craft beers, fine wines and delicious restaurant food. We think you’re going to have a great time. To help out with this take a look at the FAQ section.
 
2018 marks the 5th Annual South Bay Beer & Wine Festival, and we want to thank you for your support and attendance. We are also grateful to more than 80 Participants that bring all the taste treats. We applaud their generosity! You can view all these great providers in the Participants section. Take a moment between tastes to make a note or two right here in this app, or mark down a rating. That way tomorrow, or even months from now you’ll remember what you liked. Beer & Wine festivals are not conducive to accurate memories.
 
This event is a fundraiser hosted by the Rotary Clubs of Palos Verdes Peninsula and South Bay Sunrise (in Torrance). Please take a look at our Organizations We Support section to learn what you are contributing by way of your attendance at this event.

Enjoy the day, and know you are doing more than having a good time. You are helping to support the communities of the South Bay, Peninsula and L.A. Harbor. Thank you.
